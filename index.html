Teaching Mission

Every project is written like a mini lesson: beginner‑friendly explanations, commented code, reproducible results, and practical impact on trading or risk. The goal is to make high‑quality quant learning accessible for any motivated student with a laptop and curiosity.

Open‑education focus. All materials are MIT‑licensed so under‑resourced schools can reuse, remix, and translate freely.
