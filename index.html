Projects

#01

Momentum vs Mean‑Reversion

Built a pipeline to compare momentum and mean‑reversion signals under transaction costs. Designed walk‑forward evaluation for robust testing. Tuned lookback windows and studied parameter sensitivity. Tracked hit‑rate, Sharpe, and max drawdown to judge realism. Produced clear plots and a short PDF report summarizing takeaways.

#02

Portfolio Optimization with Efficient Frontier & Risk Parity

Reproduced Modern Portfolio Theory on a small multi‑asset universe. Estimated mean/variance/covariance and traced the efficient frontier. Implemented max‑Sharpe and risk‑parity allocations with constraints. Visualized weight dispersion and frontier curvature. Benchmarked allocations against equal‑weight portfolios.

#03

Pairs Trading: Cointegration & Z‑Score

Researched cointegrated pairs and built a z‑score mean‑reversion engine. Included Engle‑Granger tests, position sizing, slippage/costs, and basic risk controls. Showed equity curves and trade distributions. Evaluated sensitivity to entry/exit thresholds. Documented a clean notebook with all stages.

#04

Options Volatility — Smile & Surfaces

Used synthetic options data to compute implied vols, smiles, and a surface. Benchmarked a covered‑call P&L as a sanity check. Visual assets help explain skew and term structure. Wrote concise READMEs to make the steps reproducible. Suitable as a teaching piece for options intuition.

#05

Machine Learning for Markets

Built a small ML pipeline (features → model → evaluation) on synthetic SPY‑like data. Compared equity growth of the model vs benchmarks. Reported ROC/AUC and error trade‑offs under class imbalance. Stored reproducible metrics.json for transparent scoring. Emphasis on clarity over complexity.

#06

Sharpe Ratio Optimization

Optimized allocations to maximize Sharpe using a simple returns/prices dataset. Visualized return‑volatility trade‑offs and final portfolio weights. Ensured robust cov estimation and constrained weights. Reproduced results with a clean README and CSV inputs. Good bridge between theory and practice.

#07

Monte Carlo Option Pricing

Implemented Monte Carlo paths for vanilla option pricing. Demonstrated convergence of estimated prices and distribution of terminal values. Stored sample simulated paths for transparency. Clear plots reveal variance behavior vs number of simulations. The notebook is education‑ready.

#08

Value at Risk (VaR) & Expected Shortfall

Computed historical VaR and Expected Shortfall on returns. Visualized loss distributions and tail risk behavior. Exported metrics to JSON for auditing. Complete, minimal, and easy to extend to parametric methods. Centered on interpretability for newcomers.

#09

CAPM & Fama‑French Factor Models

Estimated CAPM and Fama‑French 3‑factor regressions on synthetic factors/returns. Plotted fit diagnostics and cumulative returns. Reported betas, residuals, and model quality. Packaged data and metrics for checkability. A compact intro to factor modeling.

#10

Daily Returns & Volatility Toolkit

Built a tiny toolkit to analyze daily returns, drawdowns, and rolling volatility/Sharpe. Included price series and histogram visualizations. Exported summary metrics for convenient comparison. Meant as a first lab for students. Clear plots, zero dependencies beyond basics.
